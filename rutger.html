Rutger

Italian Greyhound

2-year-old male. Rutger used to live with a famous dog trainer, but after being bitten by one of the 'Goldfangs', gets persuaded to join the villains.

Rutger's greatest strength is unarguably speed. In battles, Rutger gains tremendous force by running at full speed and strikes his enemy with 'Sonic Boom'.
