About

RetroBot CI

RetroBot is a Korea-based 3D CGI studio known internationally for TOBOT and PotenDogs. We specialize in the complete production pipeline — from concept and world-building through animation, post, and global distribution.

With a team built around creative and technical excellence, RetroBot has established a reputation for delivering high-quality animated properties that resonate with audiences worldwide.

Dahl Lee

CEO · Animation Director · Songwriter

Founded RetroBot in 2008 with a vision to build world-class animated IPs from Korea. Has directed multiple internationally distributed series and feature productions, while also contributing original music to RetroBot's properties.
